Meet Ceci

Ceci Touzón is a bilingual Cuban-American theatre artist from Raleigh, NC. Her passion led her to Oklahoma City University where she pursued a BA in Theatre Performance and in Drama Education with minors in Children’s Theatre and Directing. She wants to spread the magic of storytelling in any way that she can so that theatre may impact others the way it has impacted her!�

Some of her favorite credits include Tomás and the Library Lady, In the Heights, and She Kills Monsters. �Most recently, she co-directed Mamma Mia! at Classen School of Advanced Studies at Northeast.�
